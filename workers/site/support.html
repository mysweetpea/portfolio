Why crypto? We prioritize privacy. Crypto donations (especially Monero) are anonymous, irreversible, and require no personal information. No KYC, no chargebacks, no paper trail.
Why do you need donations? ▼

Running these services costs money — server hardware, electricity, internet bandwidth, and domain registration. Donations help cover these costs and fund future improvements and new services. We do not run ads or sell your data.

Why don't you accept credit cards? ▼

We prioritize privacy. Credit card payments require sharing your personal and financial information with payment processors, which creates a paper trail linking your identity to our services. Cryptocurrency donations (Monero and Bitcoin) allow us to maintain a privacy-first approach with no KYC requirements, no chargebacks, and no third-party data collection.

Is my donation anonymous? ▼

Yes. Crypto donations are anonymous by default. Monero (XMR) is fully anonymous — sender, receiver, and amount are all hidden. Bitcoin (BTC) is pseudonymous — your wallet address is visible but not linked to your identity.

Can I get a refund? ▼

Crypto donations are irreversible. If you have a genuine issue, contact support@mysweetpea.cc.
