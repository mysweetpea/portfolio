FAQ

Why do you require a donation before access? ▼

The donation requirement helps prevent abuse and spam. Without it, anyone could create unlimited accounts, overload the server, and ruin the experience for everyone. A small contribution ensures that users are genuine and invested in the community.

Why do you need donations? ▼

Running these services costs money — server hardware, electricity, internet bandwidth, and domain registration. Donations help cover these costs and fund future improvements and new services. We do not run ads or sell your data.

Why don't you accept credit cards? ▼

We prioritize privacy. Credit card payments require sharing your personal and financial information with payment processors, which creates a paper trail linking your identity to our services. Cryptocurrency donations (Monero and Bitcoin) allow us to maintain a privacy-first approach with no KYC requirements, no chargebacks, and no third-party data collection. This aligns with our core values of self-hosting and data sovereignty.

How do I donate? ▼

We accept Monero (XMR) and Bitcoin (BTC). Visit the donation form to see our wallet addresses.

What happens after I donate $5? ▼

Fill out the donation form with your details and transaction hash. We verify your donation and email you an invite code. Use the code to create your account.

Is this a subscription? ▼

No. A single donation grants lifetime access. There are no recurring fees or billing cycles.

How do I get Sweet Pea tier? ▼

Sweet Pea tier is limited due to resource constraints. Contact support@mysweetpea.cc to inquire about availability and pricing.

Can I get a refund? ▼

Crypto donations are irreversible. If you have a genuine issue, contact support@mysweetpea.cc.
