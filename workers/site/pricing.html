FAQ

How do I donate? ▼

We accept Monero (XMR) and Bitcoin (BTC). Visit the donation form to see our wallet addresses.

What happens after I donate $5? ▼

Fill out the donation form with your details and transaction hash. We verify your donation and email you an invite code. Use the code to create your account.

Is this a subscription? ▼

No. A single donation grants lifetime access. There are no recurring fees or billing cycles.

How do I get Sweet Pea tier? ▼

Sweet Pea tier is limited due to resource constraints. Contact support@mysweetpea.cc to inquire about availability and pricing.

Are donations tax-deductible? ▼

No. Contributions are not tax-deductible. They help cover server hosting costs and future improvements.

Can I get a refund? ▼

Crypto donations are irreversible. If you have a genuine issue, contact support@mysweetpea.cc.
